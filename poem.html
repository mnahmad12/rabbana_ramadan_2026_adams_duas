About This Poem

This poem are the duas from the ADAMS Ramadan 2026 fajr khatirah series as found on rabbanaduas.com versified by Ustadh Shad Imam. Our Lord! Accept from him and his family!

↓

Oh Allah guide us upon the Straight Path,
the Path of who carry the day, not of those who have gone astray.

Rabbana accept from us our deeds, every night of every year,
for You are the One who Knows all, and You are the One who hears.

Rabbana Grant us goodness in this World and the Next,
Protect us from the Fire, Make us of those who are Blessed.

Rabbana Shower us with perseverance, make firm for us our steps,
Grant us victory over the disbelievers, from the Generosity that You give.

Rabbana Don’t let our hearts waiver, once faith has taken hold,
Grant us from Your Mercy, Forgive our sins, new and old.

Rabbana Perfect for us our Lights, Forgive our sins and excesses,
Grant us Offspring that are righteous, make them people who are blessed.

Rabbana You did not create us without purpose, we see Your Signs in all creation.
Glorified are you always, in every time, space and station.

Rabbana Provide for us our Rizq, You are the Best of those that provide,
We have wronged ourselves immensely, From You we cannot Hide.

Rabbana Judge between us in Truth, You are the Best of those who Judge,
Allow us a blessed landing, Deliver us by Your Mercy from Above.

Rabbana make us and our children of those who pray,
And accept from us our worship, Guide us along the way.

Rabbana Have mercy on our parents, they took care of us when we were young,
Grant them the best of endings, with the Shahadah on their tongues.

Rabbi Grant me an entrance of honor, and an exit just the same,
Grant me Support from none other than You, so that I begin in Your Name.

There is no God except You! As was said in the belly of the Whale,
Glorified are You - Above anything the world entails.

Rabbi expand for me my chest, make easy for me my task,
Untie the knots from my tongue, make the disbelievers understand my asks.

Rabbi, increase us in knowledge, make us of those guided aright,
We submit to Your Will, ya Allah, we ask You for Your Light.

Rabbana bless us with spouse, children and family, that will be the comfort of our souls,
And make us leaders of all righteousness, and Grant us the courage to be bold.

Rabbana do not place upon us a burden that we cannot bear,
Pardon us and forgive us, in You we put our Trust and Care.

Do not let our hearts waiver, once you have given us a taste of Faith.
Remove from hearts any ill-feelings, fill our lives with Love, not Hate.

Rabbi, Inspire us to be gracious, and always full of praise and thanks.
You have blessed us, and the ADAMS community, we ask You, Please ya Rabb, to raise us in our ranks.

Ameen

ADAMS RAMADAN 2026 Duas
